How to Use This Website

  • Search: Use the Search page to look up outbreaks or patients by disease, region, or date.
  • Update: Go to the Upload page to insert new outbreak or patient data into the database.
  • Delete: Use the Delete function to remove incorrect or outdated records.
  • Export: After searching, export results as CSV for offline use.
  • Disease Details: View detailed information about each disease and its symptoms.
